Frequently asked questions

How fast can we start?
14 days. Day 1 is onboarding. Days 2-7 are setup + warmup. Day 7+ we start sending. Some clients book their first meeting in week 2.
Do I have to write the emails?
No. We write them. You approve them on day 5 before launch. Most clients approve 80%+ of drafts as-is.
What if my open rates drop?
We monitor daily. If open rates fall below 40%, we pause, diagnose, and adjust (rotate subject lines, swap domains, refresh list). We've never had a client lose their sending reputation.
What if I get more replies than I can handle?
Great problem. We can adjust sequence volume down, or you can hire. Either way, that's success — we've never had a client fire us for booking too many meetings.
Do you sign an NDA?
Yes, on request. Standard mutual NDA, signed within 24 hours.
What's your contract length?
Month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We keep clients because it works, not because they're locked in.
How do you measure success?
Reply rate (>5%), positive reply rate (>1%), meetings booked (5-15/mo). We report weekly with a full markdown breakdown of every campaign.
Will this hurt my domain?
No — we use separate sending domains that we own and warm up for 2 weeks before any campaign. Your main domain is never at risk.
