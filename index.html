Frequently asked questions

How fast will I see results?
Most clients book their first qualified meeting within week 2 of launch. By month 2, you're seeing consistent 5-15 meetings per month. By month 3, the system is fully optimized for your specific ICP and offer — typically booking 10-20 meetings per month at that point.
Do I have to write the emails myself?
No. We write the full 4-email sequence in your voice. You review and approve before launch. Most clients approve 80%+ of our drafts as-is. If you want to tweak tone or specific claims, we revise until you're happy.
What if I get more replies than I can handle?
That's a great problem. We'll adjust the campaign volume down, or you can hire more AEs. Either way, the system is working — we're bringing you more demand than you can close, which is the entire point.
What's your contract length?
Month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We keep clients because the work delivers results, not because they're locked in. No setup fees.
How do you measure success?
Three numbers: reply rate (we guarantee above 5%), meetings booked (5-15 per month depending on tier), and pipeline generated (the $ value of the meetings, which we report monthly). We share every metric transparently in your weekly report.
Will this hurt my domain reputation?
No. We use separate sending domains that we own and manage, with proper SPF/DKIM/DMARC authentication and a 14-day warmup before any campaign goes live. Your main domain is never used for cold outreach and stays clean.
What makes OutboundSolved different from other agencies?
Three things: (1) We combine senior B2B expertise with AI-augmented operations, so you get the quality of a senior operator at a fraction of the cost of a traditional agency. (2) Our reply SLA is 1 hour, not 24-48 hours like most agencies. Speed of response is the single biggest factor in close rate. (3) We don't hand your account off to whoever's available — same operator, every month, deeply familiar with your business.
What if I want to cancel?
Email us, 30 days notice, done. No fees, no clawback, no awkward "are you sure?" calls. We keep clients because the work delivers results, not because contracts trap you. If we're not earning our fee, you should leave.
