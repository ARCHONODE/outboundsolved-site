## CATEGORY 1: SAAS → SAAS (15 emails)

### Email 1: The "We Do What You Do" Pattern
**Subject:** quick question, {{first_name}}

**Body:**
Hey {{first_name}},

Noticed {{company}} is doing outbound for SaaS. We're building something similar at {{your_company}} — figured you'd be the right person to ask.

How are you handling list building + email copy at scale? We've been struggling with consistency.

**Why it worked:** Peer-to-peer outreach. Lower barrier than vendor-to-buyer. The "struggling with" admission creates instant common ground.

For each email, ask yourself:

1. **What's the opener hook?** (Pattern interrupt, observation, value drop, etc.)
2. **What pain are they solving?** (Be specific to their role/industry)
3. **What's the proof point?** (Specific number, case study, social proof)
4. **What's the CTA?** (Demo, call, reply, download)
5. **Why would they respond?** (What's in it for them?)
