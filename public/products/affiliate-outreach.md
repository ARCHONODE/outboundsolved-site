# Affiliate Outreach Playbook
## How to Find, Pitch, and Close Affiliate Partners

*For B2B SaaS founders, e-commerce brands, and course creators. The complete system for building an affiliate program that drives 20-40% of revenue without paid ads.*

- ✅ Performance-based (you only pay for results)

**This playbook solves all of that.**

## PART 1: THE AFFILIATE PROGRAM FUNDAMENTALS

### What is an Affiliate Program?

**An affiliate program** is a partnership where:
- You (the merchant) offer a commission to partners (affiliates)
- Affiliates promote your product to their audience
- They earn commission for each sale they generate
- You get customers you wouldn't have reached otherwise

**Common structures:**
- **SaaS:** 20-30% recurring commission (paid monthly for as long as the customer stays)
- **E-commerce:** 10-20% one-time commission
- **Courses:** 30-50% one-time commission
- **Services:** 10-20% one-time commission
- **Physical products:** 5-15% one-time commission

### Why Most Programs Fail

**The 5 main reasons:**

1. **Bad commission** — too low to motivate, too high to be profitable
2. **Bad product** — affiliates can't promote something they don't believe in
3. **No resources** — affiliates don't have swipes, images, landing pages
4. **No support** — affiliates get ignored once they sign up
5. **No promotion** — you don't promote your affiliate program

**This playbook solves all 5.**

### The Affiliate Math

**Example: B2B SaaS with $97/mo product, 30% recurring commission**

- Average customer lifetime: 12 months
- Average customer value: $97 × 12 = $1,164
- Affiliate commission per customer: $1,164 × 30% = $349
- Your profit per customer: $1,164 × 70% = $815

**If an affiliate drives 10 customers, they earn $3,490. You earn $8,150.**

**Win-win.** They get high commission for promoting a product they believe in. You get customers without paid ads.

## PART 2: DESIGNING YOUR PROGRAM

### The Commission Structure

**Factors to consider:**
- Your profit margin
- Customer lifetime value
- Industry standards
- Affiliate motivation
- Competitive positioning

**SaaS Commission Tiers:**

**Tier 1: Basic (20% recurring)**
- For most affiliates
- Easy to join
- Good for high-volume affiliates

**Tier 2: Pro (30% recurring + bonus)**
- For affiliates who drive 10+ customers/month
- Bonus for hitting milestones
- Early access to new products

**Tier 3: Elite (40% recurring + custom)**
- For top 5% of affiliates
- Custom landing pages
- Dedicated affiliate manager
- Higher payouts

**Example Tier Structure:**
- 0-10 sales/month: 20% recurring
- 11-50 sales/month: 25% recurring
- 51+ sales/month: 30% recurring + performance bonuses

### Cookie Duration

**Cookie duration** = how long after a click the affiliate gets credit for the sale.

**Standard durations:**
- 30 days (most common)
- 60 days (better for high-ticket products)
- 90 days (best for enterprise B2B)
- Lifetime (rare, usually for high-margin products)

**My recommendation:** 60-90 days for B2B SaaS. Longer cookies = more affiliate motivation.

### Payment Terms

**When to pay affiliates:**
- Monthly (most common — NET-30 after the month's end)
- Per sale (less common, harder to manage)
- Threshold-based (minimum $50-100 to withdraw)

**My recommendation:** Monthly, NET-30, with a $50 minimum threshold.

### Terms & Conditions

**Must cover:**
- Spam policy (no spam allowed)
- Brand guidelines (how they can use your brand)
- Payment terms (when, how, minimum)
- Cookie duration
- Commission structure
- Refund policy (usually commissions reversed on refunds)
- Termination clause (you can remove affiliates for violations)
- FTC compliance (affiliates must disclose the relationship)

## PART 4: FINDING AFFILIATES

### The Best Affiliate Sources

**Tier 1: Your Existing Network**
- Current customers who love your product
- Past customers
- Friends in the industry
- Colleagues
- People who have mentioned your product positively

**Tier 2: Content Creators**
- Bloggers in your niche
- YouTubers
- Podcasters
- Newsletter operators
- Twitter influencers
- LinkedIn thought leaders

**Tier 3: Complementary Products**
- Products that don't compete with yours but serve similar customers
- Tools your customers use
- Services that complement yours
- Courses that teach related skills

**Tier 4: Affiliate Networks**
- ShareASale
- CJ Affiliate
- Impact Radius
- Awin

### Finding Affiliates in Your Niche

**Method 1: Google Search**
1. Search: "{{your_topic}} blog"
2. Search: "{{your_topic}} review"
3. Search: "{{your_topic}} podcast"
4. Look for content creators
5. Check if they have affiliate links
6. Reach out

**Method 2: Social Media Search**
1. Twitter: Search for "{{topic}} tips" or "{{topic}} review"
2. LinkedIn: Search for "{{your_role}}" + "{{topic}}"
3. Instagram: Search for relevant hashtags
4. YouTube: Search for "{{topic}} tutorial"

**Method 3: Competitor Affiliate Programs**
1. Find your competitors' affiliate programs
2. Look at who's promoting them
3. Reach out to those affiliates
4. Offer better terms

**Method 4: Affiliate Networks**
1. Join networks like ShareASale, CJ, Impact
2. Search for affiliates in your category
3. Reach out to active ones

**Method 5: Your Customers**
1. Identify your happiest customers
2. Reach out with affiliate offer
3. They're already sold on the product

### Qualifying Affiliates

**Not everyone is a good affiliate. Look for:**

✅ **Audience size:** 1K+ followers (more = more potential)
✅ **Engagement rate:** 2%+ on social, 20%+ email open rate
✅ **Content quality:** High-quality content in your space
✅ **Audience fit:** Their audience matches your target customer
✅ **Credibility:** Trusted voice in the niche
✅ **Track record:** Has successfully promoted other products

**Red flags:**
- ❌ Low engagement (bought followers)
- ❌ Spammy content
- ❌ Promotes too many products
- ❌ Audience doesn't match
- ❌ Negative reputation

### The Affiliate Scorecard

**Rate each prospect 1-10 on each:**

- Audience size: __/10
- Engagement rate: __/10
- Content quality: __/10
- Audience fit: __/10
- Credibility: __/10
- Track record: __/10

**Total: __/60**
- **50-60:** Top-tier affiliate, reach out immediately
- **40-49:** Good affiliate, reach out
- **30-39:** Average, may be worth a try
- **Below 30:** Skip, not worth the time

## PART 8: MANAGING AFFILIATES

### The 80/20 of Affiliate Management

**20% of affiliates drive 80% of results.** Focus on your top performers.

**Tier 1: Top Performers (Drive 80% of sales)**
- Personal check-ins monthly
- Custom commission rates (higher)
- Exclusive offers
- Early access to new products
- Co-marketing opportunities

**Tier 2: Active Affiliates (Drive 15% of sales)**
- Monthly newsletter
- Quarterly check-ins
- Standard commission
- Access to all resources

**Tier 3: Inactive Affiliates (Drive 5% of sales)**
- Re-engagement campaigns
- Special "come back" bonuses
- Lower priority

### Monthly Affiliate Newsletter

**Send to all affiliates, monthly:**

**Sections:**
1. **Top performers** (recognition + case study)
2. **New resources** (swipe copy, creative assets)
3. **Product updates** (new features, improvements)
4. **Tips & best practices** (what's working now)
5. **Upcoming promotions** (limited-time offers, contests)
6. **Q&A** (answer common questions)

### Re-Engagement Campaign

**For inactive affiliates (90+ days no sales):**

**Email 1: The Check-In**
"Hi {{name}}, I noticed you haven't been active lately. Everything okay? Let me know if you need any support or new resources."

**Email 2: The Incentive (7 days later)**
"Hi {{name}}, as a thank you for being part of our program, we're offering a special bonus: 2x commission for your next 3 referrals. Want to give it another try?"

**Email 3: The Breakup (14 days later)**
"Hi {{name}}, I haven't heard back. I understand if you've moved on. Should I close your affiliate account, or would you like to stay on our list for future updates?"

## PART 10: COMMON MISTAKES

### Mistake 1: The Low Commission
❌ 5-10% commission (too low to motivate)
✅ 20-30% recurring (motivates long-term promotion)

### Mistake 2: The Bad Product
❌ Promoting a product you don't believe in
✅ Only recruit affiliates who actually use and love your product

### Mistake 3: The No-Resources Mistake
❌ Sign up affiliates, then leave them to figure it out
✅ Provide swipe copy, banners, case studies, everything they need

### Mistake 4: The No-Support Mistake
❌ Ignore affiliates once they sign up
✅ Monthly newsletter, Slack group, dedicated manager

### Mistake 5: The One-Time Setup
❌ Set up the program once, never touch it again
✅ Continuously recruit, support, and optimize

### Mistake 6: The No-Incentives Mistake
❌ Same commission for everyone
✅ Tiered commission based on performance

### Mistake 7: The Bad Tracking
❌ Affiliates can't see their stats
✅ Real-time dashboard with clicks, conversions, earnings

### Mistake 8: The Slow Payouts
❌ Pay out quarterly
✅ Pay out monthly (NET-30 is standard)

### Mistake 9: The No-Recognition
❌ All affiliates are anonymous
✅ Monthly "top affiliate" recognition, case studies

### Mistake 10: The Spammy Affiliates
❌ Allow anyone to join, no vetting
✅ Vet affiliates before approving, enforce quality standards

## PART 11: ADVANCED STRATEGIES

### Strategy 1: The "Super Affiliate" Program

**Identify your top 5% of affiliates and give them:**
- Higher commission (40-50% vs 20-30%)
- Custom landing pages
- Direct access to your team
- Early access to new products
- Co-marketing opportunities
- Quarterly in-person meetups (optional)

**Why it works:** Super affiliates can drive 50%+ of your total affiliate revenue. Treat them like VIPs.

### Strategy 2: The Product Launch Sequence

**When launching a new product:**
1. Give existing affiliates 2-week early access
2. Send them "insider" content (behind-the-scenes)
3. Let them be the first to promote
4. Offer bonus commission for launch week (2x or 3x)
5. Create a "launch leaderboard" with prizes

### Strategy 3: The Content Partnership

**Beyond just affiliate links, partner with creators on content:**
- Joint webinars
- Co-authored blog posts
- Podcast guest appearances
- Collaborative case studies
- Bundle deals (your product + their product)

### Strategy 4: The Affiliate Contest

**Run quarterly contests:**
- "Top affiliate of Q1" gets $5K bonus
- "Most creative promotion" gets featured
- "First to 50 referrals" gets lifetime 50% commission
- Gamify with leaderboards

### Strategy 5: The Tiered Program

**Beyond basic vs. pro, create multiple tiers:**

**Tier 1: Starter (20% recurring)**
- Easy to join
- Standard resources

**Tier 2: Pro (30% recurring)**
- Requires 10+ sales/month
- Premium resources
- Priority support

**Tier 3: Elite (40% recurring)**
- Requires 50+ sales/month
- Custom landing pages
- Dedicated manager
- Co-marketing budget

**Tier 4: Partner (50% recurring)**
- Requires 200+ sales/month
- Equity opportunities
- Quarterly retreats (if applicable)

## PART 12: MEASURING SUCCESS

### Key Metrics to Track

**Program-level:**
- Total affiliates
- Active affiliates (last 30 days)
- Total revenue from affiliates
- % of total revenue from affiliates (target: 20-40%)
- Average revenue per affiliate
- Top 10 affiliates' share of total

**Affiliate-level:**
- Clicks per affiliate
- Conversion rate per affiliate
- Revenue per affiliate
- Commission paid per affiliate

**Channel-level:**
- Which affiliate types perform best (bloggers vs. podcasters vs. YouTubers)
- Which content types drive the most sales
- Which traffic sources convert best

### Calculating Affiliate ROI

**Cost of program:**
- Affiliate software: $99/mo
- Affiliate manager (if full-time): $5K/mo
- Time spent on program: 10 hrs/mo × $100/hr = $1K
- Total: $6.1K/mo

**Revenue from program:**
- 100 affiliates drive 50 sales/mo
- Average customer value: $1,164/year
- Annual revenue: $50K × $1,164 = $58.2K/year = $4.85K/mo

**ROI:** ($4.85K - $6.1K) / $6.1K = -20% (negative)

**Wait, that's bad.** Let me redo with realistic numbers.

**Realistic scenario:**
- 100 active affiliates (top 20%)
- 200 sales/mo total
- $1,164 avg customer value, 12 month avg lifetime
- Monthly revenue: $23,280
- Total cost: $6.1K

**ROI:** ($23,280 - $6,100) / $6,100 = 281% (very good)

**The difference: Number of active affiliates. Focus on quality, not quantity.**

## PART 13: THE 90-DAY AFFILIATE PROGRAM LAUNCH PLAN

### Pre-Launch (Days 1-30)

**Week 1-2: Set up the infrastructure**
- Choose affiliate software (Rewardful, etc.)
- Create affiliate landing page
- Set up commission structure
- Create terms & conditions
- Set up payment system

**Week 3-4: Create resources**
- Swipe copy (5-10 templates)
- Creative assets (banners, images)
- Product information
- FAQ document
- Tracking dashboard

**End of Pre-Launch:** Invite your first 10 affiliates (existing customers, friends)

### Launch (Days 31-60)

**Week 5-6: Recruit initial affiliates**
- Reach out to 20-30 potential affiliates
- Personalize each pitch
- Follow up consistently
- Aim for 10-15 sign-ups

**Week 7-8: Onboard and support**
- Send welcome emails with resources
- Host a "getting started" webinar
- Create a Slack group or community
- Provide 1-on-1 support for top affiliates
- Share early results and wins

**End of Launch:** 10-15 active affiliates, first sales coming in

### Growth (Days 61-90)

**Week 9-10: Optimize and scale**
- Identify top performers, give them more support
- Identify underperformers, offer help or remove
- Refine commission structure based on data
- Create more resources based on what affiliates need
- Run first contest or promotion

**Week 11-12: Scale what works**
- Recruit 20-30 more affiliates
- Double down on the channels that work (bloggers, podcasters, etc.)
- Launch a "super affiliate" tier
- Add new resources (case studies, video scripts, etc.)

**End of 90 Days:** 30-50 active affiliates, 10-30% of revenue from affiliates
