Sample: "Too expensive" — 4 Responses

Response 1: ROI Reframe
"I hear you. Can I ask — too expensive compared to what? Most of our clients find that we pay for ourselves in the first 30 days. {{Client_name}}, for example, invested $X and generated $Y in new revenue."

Response 2: Value Comparison
"Makes sense. Let me put it in perspective: that's $X per day. Less than your daily coffee. And based on what you've told me, even ONE new client would more than cover the investment."

Response 3: Payment Plan
"Totally get it. We do offer payment plans — $X/month instead of $Y upfront. Would that work better for your budget?"

Response 4: Cost of Inaction
"Understood. Can I ask what the cost of NOT solving {{problem}} is? If it's costing you $Y per month, then our solution pays for itself in {{timeframe}}."
