Frequently asked questions

Do I need LinkedIn Sales Navigator?

Recommended but not required. Sales Navigator gives you better search filters. The templates work with regular LinkedIn too.

Are these for B2B only?

Yes. All templates assume B2B prospecting (founders, SDRs, B2B sales). Not optimized for B2C influencers or content creators.

Can I use automation tools with these?

Technically yes, but we don't recommend it. LinkedIn bans accounts that use automation. Manual outreach has higher response rates.

How many connection requests per day?

20-30 per day is safe. More than 50/day risks getting your account restricted. The pack shows you how to stay under the radar.

What response rate should I expect?

With good targeting and personalization: 10-20% response rate on first message, 2-3% meeting booked rate. See the measurement section for benchmarks.
