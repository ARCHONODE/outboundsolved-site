## How to fill this template

**Hard data:** Comes from your Apollo + Instantly + Sheets data. Should be automated in week 4+ of using the scripts.

**Soft insights:** What worked, what to try next — this is where you (the human) add 2-3 sentences per section based on what you noticed in the data.

**Sent it to the client:** Email + PDF (use a tool like https://www.markdowntopdf.com/ to convert this markdown to a clean PDF).

---

## What makes this report different from competitors

Most cold email agencies send reports like this:

```
This month:
- Emails sent: 1,200
- Replies: 47
- Meetings: 4
```

**That's not a report, that's a list of numbers.** The client has no idea what to do with it.

**Our report:**
- Ties every metric to dollars (ROI, pipeline value, cost per meeting)
- Names specific companies and contacts
- Provides actionable insights ("here's what worked")
- Sets expectations for next month

The difference: clients can **forward this to their CEO or board** as proof the channel is working. That makes you look like a strategic partner, not a vendor.

**This report format is the #1 reason clients stay for 12+ months.**
