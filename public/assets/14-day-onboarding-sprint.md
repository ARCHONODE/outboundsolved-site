# 14-Day Onboarding Sprint
## What Happens After You Sign Up With OutboundSolved

---

## Day 1 — Kickoff Call + ICP Deep Dive

**What we do:**
- 30-minute kickoff call with you (the client)
- Deep dive into your ICP (who you sell to, what they care about, what triggers a sale)
- Confirm your offer (what you're selling, price points, sales cycle)
- Document your "must-have" prospect characteristics vs "nice-to-have"

**What you do:**
- Fill out a 1-page intake form (5 minutes)
- Send us 3-5 examples of past customers you LOVED (so we know who to target)

**Deliverable by end of day:**
- ICP document (1 page) — your target buyer profile, written in our format

---

## Day 2-3 — Lead List Build

**What we do:**
- Run our Apollo.io query with your ICP filters
- Pull 500-1,000 prospect records
- Verify every email (Hunter.io)
- Generate personalization icebreakers for each lead via our AI script
- Remove duplicates, junk leads, out-of-ICP records
- Final list: 300-700 clean leads (depending on how many Apollo finds)

**What you do:**
- Review the ICP doc, approve or request changes
- (Optional) Send us any "must-include" prospect lists (your CRM export, conference contacts, etc.)

**Deliverable by end of Day 3:**
- Lead list CSV with 300-700 verified prospects + personalization notes

---

## Day 4-5 — Email Sequence Draft

**What we do:**
- Write a 4-email sequence tailored to your offer + ICP
- Choose 2-3 subject lines per email (A/B test setup)
- Insert personalization variables per lead
- Write auto-replies for common objections (price, "tried before", "not now", etc.)
- Send draft for your review

**What you do:**
- Read each email, request changes (tone, specific claims, anything that doesn't sound like you)
- Approve final version

**Deliverable by end of Day 5:**
- 4-email sequence, approved and ready to deploy

---

## Day 6-7 — Domain + Inbox Setup

**What we do:**
- Purchase 3-5 sending domains (Namecheap, ~$10 each)
- Set up Google Workspace inboxes on each domain (or free Gmail if you prefer)
- Configure SPF, DKIM, DMARC records
- Set up warmup on each inbox via Instantly.ai
- Begin 14-day warmup process

**What you do:**
- Approve domain purchases
- Confirm you want Google Workspace ($7/mo per inbox) vs free Gmail

**Deliverable by end of Day 7:**
- 3-5 sending domains configured and warming
- Inboxes ready

---

## Day 8-10 — Initial Soft Launch (Test Phase)

**What we do:**
- Send 5-10 emails/day from warmed inboxes to your first batch of leads
- Monitor deliverability closely (spam folder check, open rates)
- Test 2-3 subject lines per email
- Adjust copy based on early replies (if any)
- Build out the reply-handling system (categorization + auto-replies)

**What you do:**
- Check your email 2-3x/day (we'll forward important replies to you)
- Approve any copy adjustments we propose

**Deliverable by end of Day 10:**
- 30-50 emails sent
- 5-10 replies received (typical)
- Initial reply patterns identified

---

## Day 11-13 — Ramp + Optimization

**What we do:**
- Ramp to 20-30 emails/day from warmed inboxes
- Continue A/B testing subject lines based on Day 8-10 data
- Refine auto-reply templates based on real replies received
- Calendar booking confirmed and tested (Calendly link works, you get notified on bookings)
- First discovery call typically booked by Day 11-13 (if list is good)

**What you do:**
- Show up to any booked discovery calls
- Give us feedback on lead quality ("this one is great" / "this one is junk")

**Deliverable by end of Day 13:**
- 80-150 emails sent total
- 3-8 replies received
- 1-3 discovery calls booked (typical for a healthy list)

---

## Day 14 — Full Launch + First Strategy Call

**What we do:**
- Ramp to full volume (50 emails/day across inboxes)
- Send your first official "weekly performance report" (preview version)
- 30-minute strategy call with you to:
  - Review what's working (subject lines, copy, ICP)
  - Review what's NOT working
  - Plan the next 30 days
- Hand off the "what to do weekly" guide so you know what to expect

**What you do:**
- Show up to the strategy call
- Confirm you're happy with the service (or raise concerns)
- Decide if you want to continue (you can cancel at any time with 30 days notice)

**Deliverable by end of Day 14:**
- Full campaign running at production volume
- First performance report delivered
- Strategy call completed
- You're fully onboarded

---

## Days 15-30 — Stabilization + Optimization

**What happens automatically (no work for you):**
- Emails continue sending daily (50/day from warmed inboxes)
- Replies categorized automatically (AI)
- Positive replies get auto-response with your calendar link
- Meetings booked into your calendar automatically

**What we do weekly:**
- Review all UNCLEAR replies, send tailored responses
- Check deliverability metrics (open rates, spam placement)
- Tweak copy or subject lines if needed
- Generate your weekly performance report

**What you do:**
- Show up to any booked calls (~5-10/week once stabilized)
- Reply to "yes, send me more info" requests if our AI flagged them for human review
- One 30-min strategy call with us every 2 weeks (for Growth tier) or weekly (for Scale tier)

**End of Month 1 deliverable:**
- Full monthly performance report with dollar-impact numbers
- 20-60 meetings booked (cumulative)
- 5-15 closed deals (assuming your close rate is normal)
- Clear picture of what's working and what to optimize next month

## The SLA (Service Level Agreement)

What we commit to:

- **Reply handling within 1 hour during business hours** (your timezone, 9am-6pm weekdays)
- **Weekly performance report every Monday morning**
- **Monthly strategy call** (Growth tier: bi-weekly, Scale tier: weekly)
- **Reply rate > 5%** (if we drop below this, we work for free until we fix it)
- **Positive reply rate > 1%** (same guarantee)

What we don't promise:

- A specific number of meetings per month (depends on your ICP, offer, and close rate — variables we can't fully control)
- Zero spam complaints (impossible; we aim for <0.1%)
- Specific closed deal numbers (depends on your sales process after meetings)

**If reply rate stays below 5% for 30 days, we work for free that month.** That's our skin in the game.

## Common questions about the sprint

**"What if my ICP changes mid-sprint?"**
→ We adjust. ICP document is a living file. Update it, we re-prioritize leads.

**"What if I don't like the email copy?"**
→ We revise. You approve final version. We don't send anything you haven't approved.

**"What if my calendar is full and I can't take meetings?"**
→ Pause the campaign. We can pause/resume anytime. You won't be charged for paused weeks.

**"What if I want to use my own domain instead of new ones?"**
→ Not recommended (we're protecting your main domain from any risk). But we'll discuss it on the kickoff call.

**"Can I see the leads before we send?"**
→ Yes, we share the full list on Day 3. You can remove anyone you don't want us to contact.
